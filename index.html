FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Getting your AC back on track is easier than you think — just five simple steps and comfort returns.

01

Is there a service call fee?

We charge a nominal diagnostic fee for site visits to cover travel and visual diagnosis. If you authorize the proposed solution, this diagnostic fee is fully waived from your final invoice.

02

Do you offer warranties?

Absolutely. All cooling installation, repair, and part replacement services come backed by our robust corporate warranty standards. Standard repair actions carry a 90-day comprehensive labor guarantee.

03

Do you offer regular maintenance?

We do! Our seasonal maintenance plans help extend your system’s life, improve energy efficiency, and prevent sudden costly breakdowns. Our premium maintenance packages cover comprehensive dry cleanings, compressor tuning, and leak detections.

04

Do you work on all AC brands?

Yes, our highly certified appliance experts are well-equipped to service, calibrate, and resolve critical defaults across all residential, split inverter units, multi-vane ceiling systems, and heavy industrial scale cold room systems.
